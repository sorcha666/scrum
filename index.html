Project Context & Objectives

Why a digital library management system?

A network of municipal libraries needs a unified digital platform to manage their daily operations — book catalogues, member subscriptions, loans, reservations and transfers between branches. The system must support multiple user roles with secure, role-based access.

Digitize Operations

Replace manual processes with a unified web platform covering all library branches.

Secure Access

Role-based authentication for Staff, Librarians, Central Service & Readers.

Agile Delivery

Structured into 4 Sprints using Scrum methodology with strict velocity control (≤32 SP).

Case Study Overview

Catalogue

Manage books, authors, publishers, editions, prices and genres.

Loans & Returns

Borrow/return tracking, 3-book limit enforcement, late fees.

Transfers

Inter-branch book transfers, deficit detection, transfer slips.
